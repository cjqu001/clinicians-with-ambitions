Build Notes

Apr 2025 · Procedure Log

v1.2.0 — Stable Release

Procedure Log reaches stable status after 4-month beta. Longitudinal skill trajectory graphs and benchmark overlays are the headline features. 12 active clinician users. Next: iOS App Store submission.

Mar 2025 · SOAP Assistant

GPT-4o Migration Complete

AI SOAP Note Assistant migrated from GPT-4 Turbo to GPT-4o. Latency reduced by 38%, structured output compliance improved. Beta users report 20% fewer manual edits to AI-generated notes. v1.0 target Q3 2025.

Feb 2025 · DPC Portal

Async Messaging — Physician Feedback

Async messaging module received strongest positive feedback to date. Physicians report reducing phone tag by 60–70% in pilot. Two DPC practice partners requesting dedicated deployment. Scaling discussion for Q2 2025.

Feb 2025 · Imaging

Spine Measurement Tool — Architecture Decision

Decided to build standalone PyDICOM pipeline rather than plugin-based approach. Enables batch processing across full DICOM series. First use case: Cobb angle and VBQ measurement automation. MVP target Q3 2025.
